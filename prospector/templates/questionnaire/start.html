Find out if you are eligible for funding to make energy efficiency and low carbon improvements to your home

Welcome to the retrofit funding eligibility tool. You can use this tool to find out if you and your property may be eligible for funding towards the cost of energy and carbon saving improvements for your home.

If you complete this tool in full it will give you an idea how likely you will be to access funding for energy saving improvements or low carbon heating. It should take about 5 minutes.

At Plymouth Energy Community we are working with Plymouth City Council to access funding and build programmes of properties that will benefit from low carbon improvements across the city. By completing this form you are giving us consent to use your data to this end. Should you opt in, you can also ask us to get in contact with you when we think there is a suitable opportunity to provide funding for low carbon improvements. This may take some time as we will get in contact when we think we have the best chance of giving you the fullest support possible.

Use of your data

PEC will store your information securely in accordance with data protection legislation. Your information will not be kept on their system any longer than necessary. We do not ever sell data, provide it to third parties for marketing purposes or use it for fundraising.

You can correct or amend details PEC hold about you, ask to see details PEC hold about you or ask for PEC to stop storing and using your details at any time, including any data used by third parties appointed by PEC, by emailing energyteam@plymouthenergycommunity.com or calling us on 01752 477117.
