The FutureFit funding eligibility checker

We’re here to help you upgrade your home so you can stay warm and save money.

Through our partnership with Plymouth City Council, we’ve got access to money that can help you improve your home, but to get some of this you have to meet certain criteria.

We know it can be frustrating to spend loads of time on the phone, so we’ve made this handy online eligibility checker. Completing it should take around 10-15 minutes, but it means you can find out if you’re eligible for a grant with the click of a few buttons. How cool is that?

So, if you want to potentially:

  • save money
  • be warmer
  • help the environment
